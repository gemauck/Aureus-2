# Diesel refund documentation — File 1–7 taxonomy (South Africa)

Use this structure when classifying documents for diesel refund evidence bundles.

## File 1 — Regulatory and Operations Summary

**Purpose:** Licences, registrations, and high-level operational summaries.

**Include:** Mining right / prospecting rights; CIPC company registration; diesel refund registration with SARS; VAT registration certificates; environmental authorisation; summaries of operations or activities.

**Exclude:** Day-to-day fuel slips (those belong in transactional/File 3); signed supply contracts (File 2).

---

## File 2 — Contracts

**Purpose:** Legal agreements affecting fuel supply, mining services, or sale of product.

**Include:** Fuel supply contracts; mining contractor agreements; sale of product contracts; supply agreements explicitly labelled as contracts.

**Exclude:** Purchase invoices after the fact (often File 3 or 6 depending on supplier vs contractor).

---

## File 3 — Fuel System and Transactions

**Purpose:** Physical fuel infrastructure and transactional proof (supplier side).

**Include:** Tank and pump layouts/diagrams; delivery notes; supplier invoices; remittance advice; proof of payment to suppliers; **POP / PoP / EFT proof / proof of payment** (including common misspellings from OCR/file names); tank reconciliations; calibration certificates; photos of tanks.

**Exclude:** Contractor-only payment documents (prefer File 6 if clearly contractor invoices).

---

## File 4 — Assets and Drivers

**Purpose:** Lists of movable assets using rebated diesel and drivers authorised to refuel.

**Include:** Asset registers (mining / non-mining splits where used); driver lists.

---

## File 5 — FMS Data and Reports

**Purpose:** Fuel management system documentation and diesel refund quantitative evidence.

**Include:** FMS description/literature; FMS raw export data; detailed fuel refund reports; fuel refund logbooks.

---

## File 6 — Operational and Contractor

**Purpose:** Operational performance and contractor-side spend.

**Include:** Monthly survey reports; production reports; asset activity reports; contractor invoices, remittances, and proof of payment to contractors.

**Disambiguation:** If a document is clearly a **contractor** invoice or contractor POP/proof-of-payment, prefer File 6 over File 3.

---

## File 7 — Financial and Compliance

**Purpose:** Entity-level financial position and compliance anomalies.

**Include:** Annual financial statements; management accounts; VAT 201 returns; narratives of deviations, theft, or loss; fuel cap exceedance explanations.

---

When uncertain, choose the category that best matches the **primary purpose** of the document and say so with lower confidence.
