# Quick Start: Using @Mentions

## How to @Mention Someone in Comments

### Step 1: Type @
Start typing a comment and type the `@` symbol followed by a username.

### Step 2: Select from Autocomplete
You'll see a dropdown with matching users. Use:
- **Arrow keys** to navigate
- **Enter** or **Tab** to select
- **Escape** to close

### Step 3: Submit Comment
Press **Enter** (or click Send) to post your comment.

### Step 4: User Gets Notified
The mentioned user will receive:
- âœ… **In-app notification** in their bell icon
- âœ… **Email notification** (if enabled in their settings)

## How to Check Your Notifications

### In-App Notifications
1. Look for the **bell icon** in the top right header
2. **Red badge** shows unread count
3. Click to see recent notifications
4. Click a notification to go to that item

### Email Notifications
1. Check your email inbox
2. Subject: "[Person] mentioned you"
3. Click link to view the context
